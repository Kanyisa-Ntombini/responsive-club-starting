Azania's Book Club

About


This book club is a space for black women from all walks of life to explore their love of books and reading.


The topics covered in this book club range from stories told about or include black women. We also cover topics such as Pan Africanism, Black Consciousness and Intersectionality. We also explore books that may not necessarily have black women in them but that our readers like anyway.

Book of the Week


Homegoing by Yaa Gyasi


Homegoing, by Yaa Gyasi, is a historical novel that traces the descendants of two half-sisters Effia and Esi, born into different villages in Ghana. The sisters' lives follow different paths: Effia marries a wealthy Englishman and lives in Cape Coast Castle, while Esi is captured during a raid and sold into slavery.
